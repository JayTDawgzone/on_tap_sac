How accurate is this data?

Every bar & restaurant with a draft beer system in California is required to have their beer lines serviced every two weeks. When this service happens, the technician logs what beer was on tap at the time. Sac on Tap uses this data to show what's on tap at bars in the Sacramento area.

However, if the restaurant changed the beer on their line durring their two week cycle, the information could be innacurate.

COVID-19

Due to COVID-19, the company that collects the data for this application is no longer in opperation. Almost all of the bars in the Sacramento area of shut down completely or are no longer serving beer. Because of this, keeping this app updated is no longer possible.
